Physical Education Programme

MISSION & PHILOSOPHY


Dhami Sports provides quality Physical education program which is based on the integration Of international standards. Teach Sports is committed to deliver high quality physical fitness and coaching programme for different sports to educate students to be life long learners of sports which will enable them to appreciate healthy and active lifestyle and develop passion Of sports. Teach Sports believes that what individuals learn at school should have meaning and relevance in real life contexts. We promotes healthy lifestyle, physical fitness and passion of sports. We are committed to provide differentiation methods of teaching sports. Teach Sports physical education program is a well organised, sequential program that will allow the students to develop the skills, fitness, individual sports, team sports, adventure sports, movement arts and knowledge Of the healthy wellbeing. Our goal is to creat an array Of enjoyable, challenging and meaningful physical education/ Sports activities.

  • Well planned lessons incorporatinga wide range of activities
  • An emphasis on fun, enjoyment, success, fair play, self-fulfillment and personal health
  • Appropriate activities for the age and stage Of each student
  • Activities which enhance cardiovascular systems, muscular strength endurance and flexibility
  • Qualified, enthusiastic teachers
  • Creative and safe use of facilities and equipment

CAS TRIPS


Creativity, Activity, Service “Learning by Doing” is the EXPERIENTIAL learning component of the IB Diploma Programme


What are our services?

CAS Trips offer an all-inclusive travel experience for students and teachers. We plan and provide transport, accommodation, food and on-site tours at any of our destinations for your school.

Creativity – Activity – Service

The three components of CAS Trips are: creativity, activity, and service. Our CAS trips have specific elements that focus on the creative, activity and service to get real-life experience and experiential learning.
